Sentiment Analysis and Beyond in the Era of Enlarged Language Models

IJCAI 2023 Tutorial (August 20 PM)

Wenxuan Zhang, Rui Mao, Lidong Bing, Erik Cambria, Bing Liu,

▶ DAMO Academy, Alibaba Group ▶ Nanyang Technological University ▶ University of Illinois at Chicago

Abstract

In this tutorial, we aim to provide an in-depth exploration of the field of sentiment analysis and its evolution in the era of enlarged language models. We start with an overview of the traditional SA tasks and methods, followed by a discussion on the latest frontiers of sentiment analysis research. We examine emerging topics in sentiment analysis in recent years, including aspect-based sentiment analysis, conversational sentiment analysis, lifelong sentiment analysis, emotion analysis, and multimodal sentiment analysis, with a special emphasis on how pre-trained language models (PLMs) such as BERT and T5 are being used in each area.

Furthermore, we discuss the impact of large language models (LLMs) such as ChatGPT on sentiment analysis tasks and more generally, their ability to understand human emotions and affect. We examine the performance of LLMs on different sentiment analysis tasks and also their effectiveness in tackling a diverse set of tasks requiring a deep understanding of different types of human affect. Finally, we highlight the remaining challenges and future directions of sentiment analysis research in the era of LLMs.
