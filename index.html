Research Interests

I have broad interests in NLP and machine learning, especially applying deep learning for various real-wolrd NLP tasks. In particular, I focus on:
  • Question Answering and Dialogue Systems (especially in the E-commerce domain)
  • Sentiment Analysis (especially fine-grained aspect-level sentiment analysis)
I'm also interested in Transfer Learning. Many of my research involves some perspectives of TL: utilizing external supervision signals (WWW20, AACL20), the adaptation of pre-trained models (ACL21, EMNLP21), and cross-lingual transfer (EMNLP21).
