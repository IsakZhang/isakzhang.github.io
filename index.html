About me

I am currently a research scientist at Language Technology Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy. Prior to that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Wai Lam.

My research aims to advance NLP models that are inclusive, supporting diverse languages and cultures (i.e., multilingual and multicultural NLP), while also trustworthy through techniques that improve safety and robustness of language models. I am passionate about ensuring NLP benefits people equally by making systems accessible, controllable and reliable.

Some recent research projects are:
  • Understanding and enhancing multilingual ability of (mostly English-centric) LLMs: SeaLLMs
  • Ensuring LLMs' safety in both the development and deployment stages: [ICLR 2024]
  • How to comprehensively AND fairly evaluate foundation models: [NeurIPS 2023]
Feel free to drop me an email if you want to collaborate. Research intern positions are also available based in Singapore or China (Hangzhou).
