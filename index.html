About me

I am currently a research scientist at Language Technology Lab, Alibaba DAMO Academy. Prior to that, I obtained my Ph.D. degree at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, under the supervision of Prof. Wai LAM, where I conducted research on natural language processing and information retrieval.

I have broad interests in NLP and ML. I used to focus on low-resource NLP (PLM adaptation, cross-lingual/domain transfer), sentiment analysis, and conversational AI (question answering and dialog systems). Currently, I mainly work on large language models, especially on how to make LLMs more accessible in specific application scenarios.

[VALID] Research internship positions (focusing on pure research) in our group are available (base in Singapore or Hangzhou), feel free to drop me your CV if interested.
